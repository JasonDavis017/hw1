About Us

Skate Shop was founded in 2021 to make it easy for new skateboarders to find the best gear to start skateboarding. We have carefully curated a selection of skateboards and skateboarding apparel inspired by the Northern California skate scene that is perfect for beginners. If you have any questions about our assortment or need help finding the right products, send us an email.
